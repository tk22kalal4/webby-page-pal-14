RG Kar rape appeal

Court Case

Sanjay Roy, convicted in the brutal August 9, 2024 rape-murder of a postgraduate trainee at Kolkata's RG Kar Medical College, has appealed his life sentence in the Calcutta High Court; hearing scheduled for July 16, 2025.

  • The CBI and state sought a death penalty
  • Roy's defense cited "reformative and rehabilitative" grounds
Background: The 2024 incident triggered nationwide doctor strikes due to safety concerns. The former principal and a police officer were also later arrested for evidence tampering.
BMJ The Guardian

Udaipur electrocution

Campus Safety

On June 18, 2025, Dr Ravi Sharma, a senior resident at RNT Medical College Udaipur, died after electrocution from a faulty water cooler in hostel premises.

  • Resident doctors launched an indefinite strike demanding accountability and compensation
  • Strike resumed after 11 days following preliminary assurances

₹1,000 Cr scam

Corruption

CBI has exposed one of India's largest medical-college scams involving high-level officials and middlemen, allegedly receiving crores in bribes for illegal accreditations across over 40 colleges in multiple states.

  • Estimated bribery amount: ₹1,000-1,300 Cr
  • National Medical Commission (NMC) has frozen all new medical-college approvals and seat increases for 2025-26 pending investigation
Deccan Herald Medical Dialogues

Kottayam ragging horror

Violence

On February 11, 2025, five senior students at the Government College of Nursing, Kottayam, Kerala violently assaulted a junior—tying, slashing, stabbing with a metal divider and applying lotion to wounds.

  • The assault went viral via video
  • The next day an FIR was registered and five accused were arrested and suspended
